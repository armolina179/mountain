Discover Cultures of the Mountains

Welcome to Mountain Communities of the World! This webpage serves as an open resource for students, teachers, and anthropologists to explore the rich cultures that thrive in mountainous regions worldwide.

  • The Map section features an interactive map where you can click through to specific regions and discover native communities.
  • The Explore section offers a glimpse into diverse cultures with a randomized selection from around the globe.
  • The Contact section allows visitors to leave comments or suggestions for future additions to our growing library!
  • The About section dives a bit deeper into the reason why such a resource like ours is not only an exciting tool but a necessary advocate for our changing world and its people!
