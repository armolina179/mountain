About Us

Mountain Communities of the World is a website dedicated to sharing comprehensive information on mountain communities around the world. We aim to shed light on the unique cultures, traditions, and landscapes of these often overlooked communities, while highlighting their need for support and attention.

Why Mountain Communities Matter

Mountain communities are home to some of the most diverse ecosystems and cultures on the planet. They play a crucial role in maintaining biodiversity, water resources, and cultural heritage. However, these communities often face challenges such as isolation, economic hardship, and environmental threats.

Key Statistics

Focus Area Statistic
Population Over 1 billion people live in mountain regions worldwide.
Biodiversity Mountains are home to about 25% of terrestrial biodiversity.
Water Resources Mountains provide 60-80% of the world's freshwater resources.
Cultural Diversity Hundreds of indigenous languages and cultures are found in mountain areas.
Climate Change Impact Mountain regions are experiencing climate change at twice the global average rate.
